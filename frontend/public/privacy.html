Privacy Policy

Rhythmiq Personal OS

Effective Date: June 17, 2025 • Last Updated: June 17, 2025

Overview

Rhythmiq is an ADHD-aware personal operating system designed to help users manage their cognitive load, tasks, and health data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Health Data (via WHOOP Integration)

  • Recovery scores and heart rate variability
  • Sleep duration and quality metrics
  • Activity and strain data
  • Profile information necessary for health insights

Personal Productivity Data

  • Tasks and project information you create
  • Most Important Tasks (MITs) you designate
  • Ideas and notes you capture
  • Chaos level and brain weather data

Technical Data

  • Usage analytics to improve the service
  • Error logs for debugging purposes
  • API response times and system performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

Health Integration

  • Display personalized health insights on your dashboard
  • Correlate health metrics with productivity patterns
  • Provide ADHD-aware recommendations based on your readiness scores
  • Generate cognitive readiness assessments

Productivity Features

  • Organize and prioritize your tasks
  • Detect chaos patterns in your workflow
  • Provide intelligent nudges and reminders
  • Enable AI-powered assistance for planning and decision-making

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Debug technical issues
  • Optimize performance and reliability

Data Storage and Security

  • All health data is encrypted at rest and in transit
  • We use industry-standard security practices
  • Data is stored securely and access is limited to essential operations
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing

WHOOP Integration

When you connect your WHOOP device:

  • We access only the health metrics you explicitly authorize
  • Data is synced in real-time via secure webhooks
  • You can disconnect WHOOP integration at any time
  • Disconnection will stop data collection but preserve historical insights

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Disconnection: Remove integrations (WHOOP, AI services) at any time

Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

  • WHOOP: For health and recovery data
  • OpenAI/Anthropic: For AI-powered assistance (data not stored by AI providers)
  • Weather APIs: For environmental context
  • n8n: For workflow automation (self-hosted)

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Data retained for 12 months, then deleted
  • Health data: Retained according to your preferences (default: 2 years)
  • You can request immediate deletion at any time

ADHD-Specific Considerations

We recognize that ADHD users may have specific privacy concerns:

  • Cognitive Load: Privacy settings are designed to be simple and clear
  • Data Overwhelm: You control what data is displayed and when
  • Hyperfocus Protection: Built-in safeguards prevent excessive data exposure
  • Executive Function Support: Privacy controls are organized and easy to navigate

Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@rhythmiq.me
  • Website: https://rhythmiq.me/contact
  • Data Protection Officer: Available upon request

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) where applicable
  • WHOOP Developer Terms of Service
